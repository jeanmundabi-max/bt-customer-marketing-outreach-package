Common questions

The things people want to know before switching

? I'm still in contract with my current broadband provider.

You can set up EE broadband to start the day your current contract ends. We'll handle the switch. Your EE mobile plan stays exactly as it is until then — when broadband goes live, EE One benefits kick in automatically.

? Is EE broadband actually any good, or is it just the mobile they're known for?

EE won Uswitch's National Broadband Provider of the Year independently. Full Fibre means direct fibre cable to your home — not the copper hybrid that most "fibre" broadband still uses. The speeds are consistently faster and more reliable.

? My Sky TV box is set up with my current broadband. Do I lose that if I switch?

No. Sky TV is delivered via satellite separately from your broadband provider. Switching to EE broadband has no effect on your Sky TV service — it will continue exactly as before.

? Do I need to upgrade my mobile plan to get the unlimited data boost?

No. If your current EE mobile plan is £12 or more per month, your unlimited data boost activates automatically — no change to your plan, no upgrade, no extra cost. It simply unlocks when you add broadband.
